BINNING COEFFICIENTS:
RESULT_ALPHA is the value of binning the 20 datapoints for alpha's. RESULT_ZULU is the value for zulu's datapoints.
RESULT_MIDPT is the value to do the comparison with and is the average of RESULT_ALPHA and RESULT_ZULU.
  
Since across all speakers, the value of zulu after binning is greater than the value for alpha:
If greater than RESULT_MIDPT, guess zulu.
If less than RESULT_MIDPT, guess alpha.
